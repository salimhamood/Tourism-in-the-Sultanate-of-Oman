Jebel Shams

This breathtaking view showcases the majestic mountains of Jebel Shams, the highest peak in Oman's Hajar Mountain range. With its rugged terrain and stunning vistas, Jebel Shams is a popular destination for hiking and outdoor enthusiasts.

Sur

Nestled along the coast of the Arabian Sea, the picturesque village of Sur is renowned for its historic architecture, traditional dhow boat-building heritage, and pristine beaches. Visitors can explore the town's charming alleyways, visit its iconic lighthouse, or embark on a dhow cruise to witness the beauty of the Omani coastline.

Wahiba Sands

The Wahiba Sands, also known as the Sharqiyah Sands, is a vast desert expanse that spans hundreds of kilometers across central Oman. Visitors to this mesmerizing desert landscape can enjoy thrilling dune bashing adventures, camel treks, and stargazing under the clear desert skies.

Nizwa

The historic city of Nizwa is renowned for its ancient forts, bustling souks, and rich cultural heritage. Visitors can explore Nizwa Fort, browse the traditional souk for handicrafts and spices, or witness the vibrant atmosphere of the city's livestock market, known as the Nizwa Souq.
